chicken parmesan

recipe source: delicious chicken parmesan

ingredient

  • 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 4 bonless skinless chicken breast halves
  • 4 tsp. italian seasoned dry bread crumbs

equipment

  • oven
  • medium bowl
  • baking sheet

directions

  1. preheat the oven to 425 degrees
  2. combine Mayonnaise with cheese in medium bowl
  3. arrange chicken breast on baking sheet, then sprinkle with bread scrumbs
  4. bake the chicken for 20 minutes until it's throughly cooked
